## Full Description

### 🌟 About The Game
Treasure Hunt is an innovative AR location-based game that perfectly blends virtual treasures with the real world. Step outside, explore every corner, and discover precious treasures hidden in reality!

### 🎮 Core Gameplay
- **Real Map Exploration**: Based on real geography, find treasures in the real world
- **Collect Rare Items**: Discover various rare items from common to legendary
- **Achievement Challenges**: Complete collection achievements and unlock exclusive rewards
- **Leaderboard Competition**: Compete with players worldwide for the master collector title

### ✨ Game Features
- 📍 **LBS-based**: Treasures are hidden around you
- 🎁 **Daily Surprises**: New treasures refresh regularly to keep it fresh
- 🏆 **Rich Achievements**: 200+ achievements to unlock
- 💎 **Beautiful Cosmetics**: Collect avatar frames, badges and more
- 👥 **Social Interaction**: Chat, trade and share collections with friends
- 🎰 **Lucky Gacha**: Multiple pools with endless surprises

### 🔥 Why Choose Treasure Hunt?
- **Free to Start**: No payment required to start your treasure hunt
- **Healthy Activity**: Encourages outdoor exploration
- **Regular Updates**: New items and events added frequently
- **Beautiful Graphics**: High-quality game visuals for smooth experience

### 📱 Supported Devices
- iOS 12.0 and above
- Android 6.0 and above
- Devices with GPS support required

To ensure smooth gameplay, we need the following permissions:
