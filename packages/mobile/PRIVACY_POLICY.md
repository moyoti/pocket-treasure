# Privacy Policy for Treasure Cat

**Last Updated**: [Date]

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to Treasure Cat ("we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our mobile application.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Location Information
- **Precise Location**: We collect your precise geographic location to enable core gameplay features such as showing nearby treasures and allowing you to collect items at real-world locations.
- **Approximate Location**: We may use approximate location data for gameplay mechanics.
- **Purpose**: Location data is essential for the location-based gameplay experience.

### 2.2 Device Information
- Device model and manufacturer
- Operating system version
- App version
- Language settings

### 2.3 User-Generated Content
- Custom marker names and descriptions
- Display name (if chosen)
- Trade history with other players

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

- **Gameplay Features**: Enable treasure hunting, collection tracking, and trading
- **Location Services**: Show nearby treasures and validate collection actions
- **Personalization**: Remember your preferences and progress
- **Analytics**: Understand app usage to improve performance (if enabled)
- **Communication**: Send important updates about the app

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Local Storage
- **Primary Storage**: All your game data is stored locally on your device
- **No Cloud Sync**: We do not sync your data to cloud servers
- **Device Control**: You have full control over your data

### 4.2 Security Measures
- Data encryption during transmission
- Secure storage practices
- Regular security updates
- No third-party data sharing

## 5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

**We do NOT sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties.**

Exceptions (only when required):
- Legal requirements (court orders, legal processes)
- Safety protection (prevent harm to users or public)
- Business transfers (merger, acquisition, with notice to users)

## 6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

### 6.1 Access
- Request a copy of your personal data
- Review what information we have

### 6.2 Correction
- Update inaccurate information
- Complete incomplete information

### 6.3 Deletion

- Uninstall the app to remove all local data

### 6.4 Opt-Out

- Stop using the app at any time

## 7. Location Services

### 7.1 Why We Need Location
- Show treasures near your current location
- Enable collection mechanics
